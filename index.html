Chollerton Tearooms

About us

Welcome to Chollerton Tearooms, a tea room located in the picturesque hamlet of Chollerton in the North Tyne Valley. We pride ourselves on providing the highest quality service and products to all our guests.

Our tearoom offers a relaxing atmosphere where you can enjoy a delightful cup of tea or coffee, along with a wide selection of freshly baked cakes, scones etc. Our baked goods are made daily using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients to ensure that every bite is utterly delicious.

At Chollerton Tearooms, we understand the importance of customer satisfaction, which is why we go above and beyond to provide exceptional service to all our guests. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to offer recommendations and answer any questions you may have.

We also cater for special occasions and events, such as baby showers, birthdays, and hen parties. Our team will work with you to create a bespoke menu and help you plan a memorable experience for you and your guests.

We look forward to welcoming you to Chollerton Tearooms and providing you with an unforgettable experience.

Local Activites

Whether you're interested in hiking, fishing, cycling, or simply taking in the stunning scenery, there's something for everyone in this beautiful area.

One popular attraction is the nearby Kielder Water and Forest Park, which is home to the largest man-made lake in Northern Europe. Visitors can explore the park's 250 square miles of forest, take a boat trip on the lake, or even go stargazing at the Kielder Observatory.

For history enthusiasts, the local area is rich in heritage sites, including Hadrian's Wall, a world heritage site that dates back to Roman times. Visitors can explore the wall's forts, museums, and ruins, and learn about the fascinating history of this ancient monument.

And of course, guests can also explore the charming hamlet of Chollerton itself, with its quaint country lanes and stunning views of the North Tyne Valley. With so much to see and do in the local area, Chollerton Tearooms is the perfect base for your next Northumberland adventure.

How to get here

By car:

  • Start on the A1 road (also known as the Great North Road) heading north if you're coming from London or south if you're coming from Edinburgh.
  • Take the A68 exit towards Jedburgh and continue on this road for about 22 miles.
  • When you reach the village of Ridsdale, turn left onto the B6320 road.
  • Follow this road for about 5 miles until you reach Chollerton.

By bus:

  • Take a bus to Newcastle-upon-Tyne if you are coming from south or west, or to Hexham if you are coming from north or east. Both cities have a bus station and are well-connected by bus to other parts of the UK.
  • From Newcastle-upon-Tyne or Hexham, take the bus number 74, which is operated by Arriva North East, and runs between Hexham and Haltwhistle. The bus service runs seven days a week, but the frequency of buses may vary depending on the day and time. You can check the timetable on the Arriva North East website to plan your journey.
  • Get off the bus at Chollerton, which is a request stop, so you need to let the driver know in advance that you want to get off at Chollerton.

Once you arrive in Chollerton you can use a map or GPS to locate us at Chollerton Tearooms, Chollerton, Hexham NE46 4TQ, UK.
